# Welcome to the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central Catch Weights by NAV-X Help

Catch Weights by NAV-X is an app for Dynamics 365 for Business Central that enhances your Business Central experience by allowing management of your inventory in dual units of measure. It is fully integrated with Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Core Features

- **Manage inventory in dual units of measure** - Track quantities in both a base unit of measure and an alternate (catch weight) unit of measure simultaneously
- **Flexible unit of measure configuration** - Define alternate units per item with conversion relationships and rounding rules
- **Comprehensive transaction support** - Catch weight quantities are tracked across:
  - Sales and purchase documents
  - Inventory transactions and journals
  - Warehouse activities (picks, putaways, movements)
  - Item tracking for lot and serial number items
  - Transfers
- **Intelligent pricing** - Configure pricing to use either the base unit or the alternate catch weight unit for sales and purchase transactions
- **Variance management** - Handle quantity reconciliation with configurable tolerance settings
